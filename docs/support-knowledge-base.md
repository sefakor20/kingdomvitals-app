# KingdomVitals Support Knowledge Base

This document is the authoritative reference for the AI support assistant. All navigation paths, feature descriptions, and instructions are based on the actual application structure.

---

## HOW TO NAVIGATE THE APP

### Sidebar (left panel)

The sidebar is always visible on desktop. On mobile, tap the hamburger icon (top-left) to open it.

**Top section — Platform**
- **Dashboard** — your main overview page
- **Branches** — manage your organisation's branches/locations

**Middle section — Branch modules** (visible after onboarding is complete)

These are grouped into sections:

| Group | Items |
|---|---|
| Membership | Members, Households, Children |
| Visitors | All Visitors, Follow-Up Queue, Follow-Up Templates, Analytics |
| Operations | Services, Events, Attendance, Duty Rosters, Clusters |
| Assets & Care | Equipment, Prayer Requests |
| Financial | Dashboard, Donor Engagement, My Giving, Donations, Offerings, Expenses, Pledges, Campaigns, Budgets, Reports |
| Communication | SMS, SMS Templates, Email, Email Templates |
| Analytics | AI Insights, Giving Intelligence, Report Center |
| Configuration | Users, Activity Logs, Settings |

**Bottom section — Billing**
- **Subscription** — view and manage your current plan
- **Payment History** — view past invoices and payments
- **Plans** — compare and upgrade plans

**AI Assistant** — click the "AI Assistant" button in the sidebar (above the Billing section) to open this chat panel.

**Global Search** — press **Cmd+K** (Mac) or **Ctrl+K** (Windows) to search for members, events, or any page instantly.

## SUBSCRIPTION & BILLING

### Where to find it
Go to **Subscription** in the sidebar under the **Billing** section. This is NOT under Settings — it is a direct sidebar link.

### Plans available

Plan names, pricing, and limits are provided to the AI assistant from the live database — always refer to the SUBSCRIPTION PLANS section of your context for accurate, up-to-date figures. All plans support monthly and annual billing cycles, and are priced in GHS and USD.

### How to upgrade or change your plan
1. Click **Plans** in the sidebar under Billing
2. Compare available plans
3. Click the plan you want and follow the checkout steps

### Payment history and invoices
Click **Payment History** in the sidebar under Billing to view past payments and download invoice PDFs.

## MEMBERS

### Where to find it
**Membership → Members** in the sidebar.

### What you can do
- **Add a member** — click the **Add Member** button on the Members page, fill in the details, and save
- **Search members** — use the search bar at the top of the Members list
- **Filter members** — filter by status, SMS opt-out, or other criteria
- **View a member** — click on any member's name to open their profile
- **Edit a member** — on the member detail page, click Edit
- **Import members** — use the import button on the Members list; download the template first
- **Print member cards** — on the Members list, use the bulk print option; or on a member's profile, print their individual QR card
- **Invite a member to the portal** — on the member's profile, send a portal invitation so they can log in and view their own records

### Member portal
Members can log in at your organisation's URL under `/member/`. Once logged in they can view their own attendance, giving history, pledges, registered events, and household info.

## AI INSIGHTS

### Where to find it
**Analytics → AI Insights** in the sidebar. (Requires the AI Insights module — available on Professional plan.)

### What you can do
- **AI Insights Dashboard** — view smart alerts and priority action recommendations
- **Giving Intelligence** — Analytics → Giving Intelligence. AI analysis of giving trends, lapsed donors, and financial forecasts
- **Alert Settings** — AI Insights → Settings. Customise the thresholds that trigger AI alerts
- **Chatbot Monitor** — view and manage AI-powered SMS/WhatsApp conversations

### AI features that run automatically
These run in the background and power the Insights dashboard:
- Visitor-to-member conversion prediction
- Donor churn detection (flags donors inactive for 90+ days)
- AI-generated follow-up messages (require admin approval before sending)
- Attendance anomaly detection
- 4-week attendance forecasting
- Member lifecycle stage detection (new, engaged, dormant, at-risk)
- Household and cluster engagement scoring
- Financial forecasting
- Event attendance predictions
- Pledge completion predictions

## SETTINGS

### Personal settings
Access from the **user menu** at the bottom-left of the sidebar (click your name). Options:
- **Settings** → Profile, Password, Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), Appearance

### Organisation settings
**Configuration → Settings** in the sidebar. Manage branch-level settings.

### User management
**Configuration → Users** in the sidebar. Invite users to a branch and assign them a role.

### Roles available
| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| Admin | Full access to all settings, users, billing, and modules |
| Manager | Day-to-day operations; typically no access to billing or user management |
| Staff | Access to the specific modules they've been granted |
| Volunteer | Limited access to assigned tasks only |

### Two-factor authentication (2FA)
Go to your user menu → Settings → Two-Factor Authentication. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app and enter a code to enable it.

## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: I can't see a module (e.g. SMS, AI Insights) in my sidebar.**
A: That module may not be included in your current plan. Go to **Plans** in the sidebar under Billing to see what's included and upgrade if needed.

**Q: How do I switch between branches?**
A: Use the **branch selector** near the top of the sidebar (shows your current branch name with a dropdown arrow).

**Q: How do I invite a team member to the app?**
A: Go to **Configuration → Users** in the sidebar, then click Invite User. Enter their email and assign a role.

**Q: How do I give a member access to their own portal?**
A: Open the member's profile page and use the "Invite to portal" action. They'll receive an email with a link.

**Q: Where are my invoices?**
A: Go to **Payment History** in the sidebar under Billing. Click any payment to download the invoice PDF.

**Q: How do I change my plan?**
A: Go to **Plans** in the sidebar under Billing. Compare plans and click to upgrade or downgrade.

**Q: How do I enable 2FA?**
A: Click your name at the bottom of the sidebar → Settings → Two-Factor Authentication.
